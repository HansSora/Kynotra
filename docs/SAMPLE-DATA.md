## Sample Workout Program: Muscle Builder Pro

**Duration:** 12 Weeks | **Days:** 5/week | **Location:** Gym | **Level:** Intermediate

### Day 1 — Push (Chest / Shoulders / Triceps)

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Barbell Bench Press | 4 | 6-8 | 3 min | Progressive overload — add 2.5 lbs weekly |
| 2 | Incline Dumbbell Press | 3 | 8-10 | 2 min | 30° incline, focus on stretch at bottom |
| 3 | Overhead Press | 3 | 8-10 | 2 min | Standing or seated — strict form |
| 4 | Cable Flyes | 3 | 12-15 | 90 sec | Squeeze at peak contraction |
| 5 | Lateral Raises | 4 | 12-15 | 60 sec | Controlled tempo, slight lean forward |
| 6 | Tricep Rope Pushdowns | 3 | 12-15 | 60 sec | Full extension, squeeze triceps |
| 7 | Overhead Tricep Extension | 3 | 10-12 | 60 sec | Deep stretch at bottom |

### Day 2 — Pull (Back / Biceps)

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Barbell Deadlift | 4 | 5-6 | 3 min | Conventional or sumo — keep back neutral |
| 2 | Weighted Pull-Ups | 4 | 6-8 | 2 min | Full ROM, add weight when 8 reps easy |
| 3 | Barbell Rows | 3 | 8-10 | 2 min | Squeeze shoulder blades, control negative |
| 4 | Seated Cable Rows | 3 | 10-12 | 90 sec | V-bar or wide grip attachment |
| 5 | Face Pulls | 3 | 15-20 | 60 sec | External rotation at top |
| 6 | Barbell Curls | 3 | 10-12 | 60 sec | No swinging — strict form |
| 7 | Hammer Curls | 3 | 10-12 | 60 sec | Targets brachialis for arm thickness |

### Day 3 — Legs

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|----------|------|------|------|-------|
| 1 | Barbell Back Squat | 4 | 6-8 | 3 min | Below parallel, brace core tight |
| 2 | Romanian Deadlift | 3 | 8-10 | 2 min | Feel hamstring stretch, flat back |
| 3 | Leg Press | 3 | 10-12 | 2 min | Foot placement varies target muscles |
| 4 | Walking Lunges | 3 | 12 each | 90 sec | Dumbbells or barbell, full stride |
| 5 | Leg Curls | 3 | 12-15 | 60 sec | Slow eccentric for hamstring growth |
| 6 | Calf Raises | 4 | 15-20 | 60 sec | Full stretch at bottom, hold peak 2s |

### Day 4 — Upper Body

| # | Exercise | Sets | Reps | Rest | Notes |
|---|----------|------|------|------|-------|

| 4 | Cable Crossovers | 3 | 12-15 | 60 sec | Vary angle: high, mid, low |

## Gym Terminology Quick Reference

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Reps** | One complete movement of an exercise |
| **Sets** | A group of consecutive reps |
| **Progressive Overload** | Gradually increasing weight/reps/volume over time |
| **Hypertrophy** | Muscle size increase (8-12 rep range) |
| **Compound** | Exercise working multiple muscle groups (squat, bench, deadlift) |
| **Isolation** | Exercise targeting one muscle group (curl, lateral raise) |
| **Superset** | Two exercises performed back-to-back without rest |
| **Drop Set** | Performing to failure, reducing weight, continuing |
| **1RM** | Maximum weight lifted for one repetition |
| **TUT** | Time Under Tension — total duration of muscle strain per set |
| **RPE** | Rate of Perceived Exertion (1-10 scale) |
| **DOMS** | Delayed Onset Muscle Soreness (24-72 hours post-exercise) |
