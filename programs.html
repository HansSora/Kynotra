💡 Progression Tips

Aim to increase weight by 2.5-5 lbs on compound movements every 1-2 weeks. For isolation exercises, focus on mind-muscle connection and increase reps before adding weight. Rest 2-3 minutes for heavy compounds and 60-90 seconds for isolation work.
