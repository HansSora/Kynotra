Supplement Guide

Whey Protein

Essential for hitting daily protein targets. Take post-workout or between meals. 25-30g per serving.

Essential

Creatine Monohydrate

Most researched supplement. 5g daily for strength and muscle gains. No loading phase needed.

Highly Recommended

Vitamin D3

Supports immune function and bone health. 2,000-5,000 IU daily, especially if limited sun exposure.

Recommended

Omega-3 Fish Oil

Anti-inflammatory, supports heart and joint health. 2-3g EPA/DHA daily.

Recommended

Caffeine (Pre-Workout)

Boosts performance and focus. 150-300mg 30 minutes before training. Cycle on/off.
