Our Story

Kynotra was born from a simple frustration: fitness shouldn't require a $200/month personal trainer to get real results. We believed that with the right technology, anyone could access personalized, science-backed fitness programming.

Founded in 2024, we combined expertise in exercise science, sports nutrition, and artificial intelligence to build a platform that generates truly personalized workout and nutrition plans — not generic templates, but programs that adapt to your body, goals, and lifestyle.

Today, over 50,000 members use Kynotra to train smarter, eat better, and achieve results they never thought possible. And we're just getting started.
